Personnel About Us

Chief executive Officer

Say Hello to our CEO; humble leader and co-founder of Care Caterers, Precious Ndlovu. Having Realized the need for event caterers that prioritize the special, specific, and personal wishes of their clients was at an all time high, she got together with her then school mates from the Pretorian School of hospitality to establish this here organization.
She has 6 years of experience in this particular field, having started off as an intern at the prestigious catering firm: Sun Hill Caterers, the duration of which lasted 2 years. She then proceeded to attain a junior management position at Sun International Inc, working with the serving of food at the Sun City Hotels in North West - she worked there for 4 years, after which she Co-founded Care Caterers.

Head Of Business Operations

This here is our dedicated Business administrator - The man behind our well-oiled business operations; from Care Caterers' first business plan to our current business model, Lethuxolo Ntshanga has been heading our business operations from the company's conception until this day. And he has proved very resourceful in turning our establishment into one that turns a profit from satisfying every nuance of every customer's wish in terms of how they want an event handled. His credentials range from a Diploma in Human Resource from Damelin college to a Business Administration Degree from the esteemed Rosebank college. His work experience encompasses posts held in Absa Bank, Spar Supermarket and, lastly, his current position here at Care Caterers as Head Of Business Operations.

Head of Design

Pictured on the left is our esteemed Designer - The best We know, and the best you'll ever hire. A young man of great taste. All the decor designs in our catalogue were headed by him, and he has proven to be a savant in designing decors that keep on satisfying our clients. His knowledge of design stems from his 4 years as a student at the Siliva School Of Design in Bloemfontein. He also held a 1-year internship at Simmula Cateres, after which he was hired (by Simmula caterers) and worked under the esteemed Decor Queen Teresa Bopape as a junior Designer for 3 years. He then went on to Co-found Care Caterers and helped shaped it into the reputable establishment it is today
